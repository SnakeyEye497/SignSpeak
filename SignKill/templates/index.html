About Us

We are

The Team SignSpeak bridged the communication gap for individuals with speech impairments. We addresses the need for a practical and accessible solution that enables mute individuals to express themselves effectively. The rationale for this Wesite is to provide an innovative, user-friendly platform that empowers users to convert sign language into speech, allowing them to communicate more easily with the broader community. Currently, there is a significant gap in accessible communication tools for blind community, which Team SignSpeak aims to fill. Several applications are available to offer an interactive approach for beginners to learn sign languages.
